Instructions to play:

1. Select your character to bring to battle.

2. Pick your first opponent.

3. Click on 'Attack' to attack your opponent.

* This decreases your opponents HP and will also decrease yours depending on their attack.

* When your opponent blocks, it will lower your HP depending on your attack.

* When both characters block, nothing will happen.

4. When you've defeated your opponent, you will select another opponent to battle.

The goal of the game is to defeat all your opponents! Click 'Start New Game' to begin.

GOOD LUCK!!!
